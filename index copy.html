Syntō Age (the Era of Agreement) is a new epoch and an integrated system of principles and documents describing humanity’s transition to its next stage of development.

Its foundation is a Unified System of Education as a single “operating system” for everyone: a shared conceptual space, shared core knowledge, and shared values that make thinking compatible and enable genuine mutual understanding.

Its ethical engine is Hedonism of Higher Pleasure, culminating in love at the level of agápe (ἀγάπη) — creation, the transmission of knowledge, and lived participation as the highest form of pleasure.

Its social logic is the integration of humanity into a single organism, in which every person is unconditionally significant and fulfills a distinct function. Within this logic, private property ceases to be the central motivation and gives way to use, responsibility, and contribution.

Its technological basis lies in new instruments of transparency and verifiability in action and communication, reducing distortion, informational deformation, and speculation.

Syntōpolis is the first practical step within the Syntō Age — the initial form of implementing this system as a concrete structure and environment.

Syntō is the inner mechanism of conscious agreement and syntonization with this evolutionary vector.
